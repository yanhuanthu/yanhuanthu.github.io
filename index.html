I am postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Electronic Engineering of Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yong Li.

I received Ph.D. from Tsinghua University, co-advised by Prof. Depeng Jin and Prof. Yong Li.

My research is in big data analysis, spatio-temporal data mining, applied machine learning, and recommender system, with a focus on modeling novel problems and proposing effective methods in intelligient transportation.
